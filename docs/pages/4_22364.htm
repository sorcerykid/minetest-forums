[32x] Reminiscent

by Piezo_ » Mon Mar 18, 2019 10:38 pm

Reminiscent is a texture pack I made, not really with any particular style in mind that was too different from default.

Instead, for each texture, I looked at default's version, and asked myself two questions:
1. What is this texture trying to be?
2. How can I express the same thing, but give it more personality?

The result was this (very incomplete) texture pack, with a feeling to it that I can't completely describe, but I'd say is somewhere between its own kind of default, and fantasy-themed.



I'm not looking for feedback just yet, as most of the textures still haven't been finished, but I'll take advice into mind if offered.

As of now, this supports the default, and instant_ores mods.
