[Mod] Chocolate [chocolate]

by migdyn » Sun Mar 10, 2019 6:40 pm

This is a mod that adds many different kinds of chocolate.

When eaten, it gives you a number of health points depending on the type.

Planned features:
-Liquid chocolate
-Fruit chocolate
-Almond/nut chocolate
-Crafting recipes

List of things added by this mod
Dark Chocolate Bars
Milk Chocolate Bars
White Chocolate Bars
Dark Chocolate Blocks
Milk Chocolate Blocks
White Chocolate Block
Empty Glass Bottle (only if the vessels mod is not installed)
Empty Steel Bottle (only if the vessels mod is not installed)
Liquid Dark Chocolate Glass Bottle
Liquid Milk Chocolate Glass Bottle
Liquid White Chocolate Glass Bottle
Liquid Dark Chocolate Steel Bottle
Liquid Milk Chocolate Steel Bottle
Liquid White Chocolate Steel Bottle

LICENSE: MIT License

Dependencies:
vessels (optional)
