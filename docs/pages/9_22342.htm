Wings of Escape
Hello everyone!


Wings of escape return you to your spawnpoint. It's simple. You left-click with the wings and then wait 20 seconds. You can't move or fight while waiting. After that, you get teleported to your spawnpoint.

The 20-second delay is there so it can't be misused in PvP. You can whenever interrupt the teleporting by left clicking and you get your wings back. However, other players can also interrupt your teleporting so you won't flee the battle so easily.



Dependencies:
  • default
  • beds

License: MIT

Download:
  • GitLab: https://gitlab.com/h2mm/escape_wings
  • zip: https://gitlab.com/h2mm/escape_wings/-/archive/master/escape_wings-master.zip
Fun facts:
  • The model of the effect was made purely in a text editor.
  • The teleporting sound was made using my mouth and some sound filters. The other two sounds were made using improvised instruments and sound filters.
