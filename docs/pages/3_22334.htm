Its more than that line...


The basic 'story' of this park is an amusment-park-like tourist trap is run by the 'bunnyzanian' government, within the former kingdom of 'sqirrelia' who's queen is now just the governor.

So im going for a theme with 2 distinct areas: places where tourists are welcome, and the behind-the-scenes areas where everyone else lives and works, and generally keeps the park's wheels rolling smoothly.

Overall, its design is a series of train-connected 'zones' where tourists are allowed, the rest of the park's areas are 'behind the scenes', where only workers and local residents have clearance to go.

Mainly this is just one big excuse for me to build all kinds of crazy things. such as the greeter's 'office'.

Edit: the 4 buildings at the front are 'Dockyard Fortress Station' named after the Fortress right next to them. (left)
