[Mod] Cucina Vegana

by Clyde » Sun Apr 22, 2018 21:50

Hello,

Cucina Vegana is a new Mod for the vegan Kitchen in Minetest ;-). The Mod adds some new Plants like Soy, Parsley, Lettuce etc. and my Mod Vegan Sunflower is integrated too.
You can turn each plant on or off as you like.

The Oil is burnable, so you don't need coal for your Furnace.

The Mod needs only default but supports farming_redo from TenPlus, so the mod integrats in farming_redo.
If you have the mods Mobs or Animalmaterials installed, then you have recipes for Milk, (Soy)Meat and (Soy)Chicken.

With this Mod you have more freedom to create new recipes for food in Minetest.
