This server allows killing anywhere except in range of a yellow protection block. If you construct a yellow protection block and place it you will be protected in all blocks around it up to 5 blocks away. Also the protection block will prevent other users from making changes to the blocks around it, and you can use this to construct a house that can't be changed by the other players.

Near Red protection blocks you can be killed, and you will not have any bones. Your inventory will sit on the ground.

Away from protection blocks you can be killed, but you will leave a temporarily protected chest called bones with your inventory in it. For about 10 minutes only you can get things from your bones.

Don't turn your back on other players in red zones or in the wild.
