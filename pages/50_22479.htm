[Game] Alpha [Wip]

by Oil_boi » Wed Apr 03, 2019 15:55



This is my game built from scratch using textures and code from Mineclone2 and other mods/games. I'm building it from the ground up the way I feel fit. Right now it has a Minecraft Alpha feeling to it, hence why it's called alpha. It's built for Minetest 5.0

Github: https://github.com/oilboi/alpha

Live Updates: https://github.com/oilboi/alpha/commits/master

Current Features:
  • Better Sounds
  • Physical Minecarts
  • Physical Boats
  • Fun Item Collection
  • Nice Textures (Thanks to Mineclone2 Unofficial Official Textures)
  • A cool hand (Thanks to Mineclone2)
  • Item death drops
  • Suicide command /s /suicide
  • The water looks nice

Current Glitches:
  • When two items become one it resets the item age timer and then takes the normal time to collect them instead of collecting them instantly when mining.
  • That's it, there's not much here yet!

Goals:
  • Fishing?
  • Crafting!
  • Steam machinery or something?
