Coder12 wrote:...is there a crafting guide?


A crafting guide would be nice, but in the mean time, you can use the "/mods" command to look for the mod that might cover what you're trying to craft. Then google that mod to find the source and look up the crafting grids in the source. (That's what I do. :)

For example, I wanted to know how to craft a saddle. /mobs listed the "mobs_mc" mod. The first hit on google for "site:minetest.net mobs_mc mod" was https://forum.minetest.net/viewtopic.php?t=17751, which is its thread. The introductory message in that forum contained a link to its source, at https://github.com/maikerumine/mobs_mc. There, the 1_items_default.lua script contained a "register_craft" call for "mobs_mc:saddle", containing a recipe grid like this:
Code: Select all
{
     {"mobs:leather", "mobs:leather", "mobs:leather"}
     , {"farming:string", "", "farming:string"}
     , {"default:steel_ingot", "", "default:steel_ingot"}
}
