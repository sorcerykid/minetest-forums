Hello here is a new mod that has been worked on a lot! This mod adds tropical fish, coral reefs, decorative ocean blocks, and much more!

-Tropical Fish (Kob, SunStreak, and Dasher)
-Dolphin

by ShadMOrdre » Tue Apr 23, 2019 23:19

StarNinjas,

I have not yet browsed your code, but I'd like to ask now, can this be imported into lib_ecology/lib_materials? Full attribution will certainly be given.

My goals with those mods are to essentially package the best of MT into singular points of reference. One of my motivations for those mods was the incompatibility or performance of some biome generating mods. Oceans are indeed lacking MT wide. I've been able to cobble together the new default ocean life, stuff from valleys mapgen, ethereal, the Farlands game, and elsewhere, and so far have a nice growing ocean atmosphere.

An additional bonus/feature is the implementation of ecosystems. Microbiomes, if you will, to help break up the monotony of the built in biomes. Now, while traversing the map, you will encounter much more diverse flora, and coming soon, fauna.
