This post may(It may be, but it doesn't have to) be slightly outdated. If the information included in this post doesn't work for you / is irrelevant. I would recommend you reading the README.md file.
This is a mod that adds electronic cigarettes. When used, they give an effect depending on the
eliquid flavor (currently only 5 flavors exist).
Dependencies: default, playereffects, electronics(which depends on farming)

Code and Texture License: CC0
Download: https://gitlab.com/migdyn/ecigarettes/- ... master.zip
Source: https://gitlab.com/migdyn/ecigarettes


SMOKING INSTRUCTIONS
Craft an e-cigarette this way

Then, craft one of the 5 eliquids (The speed eliquid is shown in the img)
E-Liquids: Place a steel_ingot in the middle bottom and one of these ingredients in the middle:
Speed - Obsidian Shard
High Jump - Mese Crystal Fragment
Low Grav - Diamond
Anti-Grav - Diamond Block
High Grav - Mese Crystal

Then use your cigarette to smoke (VAPE?) it. It will be emptied and you will get an effect.
Happy smoking (VAPING?)
