Re: Empty Lake?

by firefox » Fri Apr 12, 2019 06:02

you need to fill the entire lake with another block/item until all water sources are replaced.
then remove the placed blocks and it will be empty.

natural lakes are entirely made of water sources, so it takes time to clear them.
playermade lakes usually have water sources only in the surface (top layer), so they can be removed faster.
