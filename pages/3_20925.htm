Secrets Inside the Box

by Wuzzy » Sat Oct 06, 2018 23:07

Inside The Box is one of my favourite servers. It also has a couple of nice secrets. In this thread I like to collect them. ALL OF THEM! :D

WARNING: For obvious reasons, this thread will contain a ton of spoilers. Do not read ahead if you do not want to get spoiled. You have been warned!

+ Soft spoilers: This only tells you what secrets there are
Soft spoiler 1: On the Main Islands (spawning area, when you join the server), there are a few small secrets and hidden areas.

Soft spoiler 2: Most Tutorial levels have hidden areas with a secret story behind them.

Soft spoiler 3: Box number 8 has an easter egg in the form of a sign.
+ Hard spoilers: All the secrets I know of so far
MAIN ISLANDS
The spawning area (which I call “Main Islands”) hold a couple of interesting secrets, and maybe not all of them have been discovered yet. I will discuss this below:

Meeting Room
At coordinates (-50, -14, 45) and (-50, -14, 25) on the Main Islands, there are doors to a locked and very dark room with a sign “Meeting room”.
These doors are shut, and there seems to be no way to enter.
Through the door you can see something like tables. From one side you can see two switches.



If you take a screenshot and jack up the brightness, you can reveal a message on a “whiteboard”:

Transcription:
Todays topics:
- erase the board after
meetings
- make difficult boxes
- clean up creative
inventory


Forest Pedestal
The Main Islands have an area with a large forest on it. If you explore it, you will quickly notice there is a strange pedestal in the middle.

It it possible to reach this pedestal by a hidden path through the leaves. Go to (-65, -23.5, 115) to find the entrance. Once inside, you can walk to the pedestal and you will see a sign. The sign has no text. Interacting with either the pedestal or sign by hand has no effect.
You can leave the forest by going to (-63, -24.5, 99) and following another path through the leaves.

I find this place quite puzzling, it raises so many questions:
  • What is the purpose of the pedestal?
  • Is there a hidden Nexus block? If yes, where can it be found? And what happens if you place it on the pedestal?
  • Are there other hidden pedestals in the Main Islands?

There are two possibilities:
  • Three is no Nexus block, and the secret ends here (or is not implemented yet).
  • There IS a Nexus block, but it's really well hidden. In that case, I am almost certain something truly crazy will happen if you put it on the pedestal. Maybe a pathway into a dungeon will open. Maybe you learn the secret which opens the Meeting Room. Maybe you will teleport you to secret box. I have no clue!

What we know for sure is that the Nexus block (if it exists) does not come from the tutorials:
sofar wrote:
Christian_Soldier wrote:Does any of the other boxes' "extra" content have anything to do with the secret pedestal in the forest biome?

You can't take any nexus blocks out of boxes, so, that would not be possible.


The Cracked Box
There is an area in the Main Islands at roughly (-13, -2, -40) that seems impossible to reach, which I call “The Cracked Box”, due to it shaped like a box with a large crack at the bottom.

You can have a glimpse into this area through holes and cracks, but nobody seems to have found a way inside yet.
It seems to be kinda connected to the broken boxes which you reach after you completed the tutorial, but even from there I failed to find a way inside. :-(

Maybe this means nothing, and it really is impossible to reach and it's just decoration. Or it might hide a real secret, who knows? If you found a way inside, please post about it, thank you very much! :D

The Nexus
In the middle, there is a large Nexus block with the text “The Nexus” written above.
The point of this remains a mystery.

There seems to be no way to interact with this block in any way. This might or might not hide a secret. Probably not. Maybe it's just decoration. But sofar seems to remain a bit secretive about this. Hmmmm …

Feel free to post your wildest speculations! :D

At least one non-Tutorial box has a little easter-egg with a hidden sign: Box number 8. If you have managed to reach the hut near the exit, inspect all 4 outside walls. There is a funny sign on one of them.

It is possible there are more easter eggs in other boxes. Boxes by sofar might have even more easter eggs, as box 8 and all tutorial boxes are made by sofar. But this is just speculation. I am not yet aware of easter eggs in boxes by other players. Or I have forgotten about them. xD

Tutorial Secrets

by Wuzzy » Wed Oct 10, 2018 12:56

I am starting to write down the first Tutorial secrets. But first, something about terminals:

+ Hints about Terminals
Terminals have some things going on that are not obvious to everyone.

You probably found a couple of locked terminals in the game. In the locked state, all commands will fail. These terminals tell you you have to type “unlock”, but it turns out you are never allowed to do that. But here's the trick: In most boxes, there is usually another way to unlock them. Anything that can trigger stuff (switches, buttons, placing a Nexus cube on a pedestal, ...) can unlock a terminal. If you managed to unlock a terminal, it will behave like a normal terminal and you can access all its files.
Note there is no guarantee that all locked terminals in a box are actually unlockable.

Terminals can also serve as triggers themselves. They trigger as soon you open them. Which means things might happen just for opening a terminal.
+ Secrets of Tutorials 1 and 2
Tutorial 1 of 8
There is a terminal in this room, but it starts locked. To unlock it, simply place the Nexus cube on the pedestal, then you can access its contents.

Tutorial 2 of 8
In one of the ways downwards there is a cul de sac with a locked terminal. It unlocks after you placed the Nexus cube.


Hint: To re-enter any tutorial, use the /enter command and the number in the list of boxes.
