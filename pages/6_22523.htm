Re: Windows 10 Minetest Malfunction

by sofar » Fri Apr 12, 2019 20:40

The screenshot shows that your device supports OpenGL 1.1, which is really old. It is likely the cause of the visual problems and I think minetest requires at least OpenGL 2.0. You can try however switching rendering engine settings, but I don't know if that will help.

Going back to an older Minetest means that you won't be able to play online in the future, since more and more servers will switch to version 5.x.x over time. However, if you play by yourself, that may be a reasonable choice in your case.

On Windows, OpenGL 1.1 is the feature level supported by the basic driver Microsoft provides.
Of course this is nowhere near sufficient for anything, so this means you need to install the right GPU drivers for you hardware (Windows might be able to do this automatically).
