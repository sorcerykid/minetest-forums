Re: Creative off, but still see inventory

by sfan5 » Mon Apr 15, 2019 3:46 pm

Make sure you haven't used /grantme all.
Doing so will grant you the "creative" privilege, making the creative inventory appear even when creative is disabled globally.

You can fix this by doing /revoke <playername> creative.
