Re: Player shown walking half under floor

by ShadMOrdre » Fri Mar 22, 2019 19:16

There are a couple of issues actually going on here.

First, update the 3D_Armor mod to use version 4.13. This is the most recent version from stu.

Second, if you also use the playeranim mod, you must ensure that it uses the latest player model.

I have noticed that with both mods active, the issue is present until both of the above steps are correct.
