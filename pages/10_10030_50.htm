"net.co - Colombia - Telmex Colombia S.A."

See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IP_addres ... _addresses


So if your friends computer isn't infested with malware that does nice rerouting stuff, then it is probably a problem with his ISP or someone behind the ISP.
