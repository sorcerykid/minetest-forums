--- Antipest [V2.2] ---


Evil sunflowers took over this area:


But now you can fight back and get free coal:


Textures: New awesome texture. A big "Thank You" goes to TM3 !!

Warning:
This mod does not care about political correctness. It uses tons of glyphosat and kills thousands
of innocent flowers. It also gives you free dirty coal which will pollute your server's atmosphere and
enhace your world's global warming ! Use at own risk !

Depends: technic, flowers

Description: adds a technic rechargeable device to kill all sunflowers in
a radius of 20 nodes and turn them into coal. Every 81 sunflowers get one coalblock,
every 9 sunflowers get one coal_lump
All can be configured in the init.lua. Self explaining. Recipe is a bit expensive I know.
I do not like being things too easy. :) It keeps also newbies from clicking around generating
lag.
Rightclick on the ground to turn chat reports on/off

[Edit]
Since version 2.1, the mod comes with a leaderboard
type "/score" to see the top ten sunflower killers
Version 2.2 supports all weeds from the nettle mod


License: License for the code: LGPL
License for all the models, textures and sound: CC BY-SA 4.0
