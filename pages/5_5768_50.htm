24/7minetester wrote:Where do you put character.x on Linux? OS: Ubuntu 15.04

character.x will not work with the current version of minetest_game unless you also modify player.lua
You can, however, use the b3d version by replacing character.b3d found in minetest/games/minetest_game/mods/default/models

The location of the minetest directory will depend on how you installed minetest.
