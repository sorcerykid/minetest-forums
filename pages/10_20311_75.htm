Multi-layered, space themed Server (but with much more than that:)

About
Survial Minetest server with hunger, vacuum and xp.
The main aspects are:
  • Space-exploration (with jumpdrive: https://forum.minetest.net/viewtopic.php?f=11&t=20073)
  • Mining on the moon (very rich on ores but hard to survive)
  • Missions (in-game creation with: https://forum.minetest.net/viewtopic.php?f=11&t=20125)
  • Experience points and statistics (Mod: https://forum.minetest.net/viewtopic.php?f=11&t=20124 Statistics: https://pandorabox.io/highscore)
  • Admin shop for ores (currency) and Player-Shops for other items

You spawn at the sky-platform above earth. On this platform is the admin-shop and the priv-building.
There are a few Teleporters up there which lead you to earth,moon or deep space.
On earth and under ground spawn dangerous monsters, best to avoid them or gear up!
Buildings on earth can be viewed in the realtime map: https://pandorabox.io/maphttps://pandorabox.io/map/#-1782.25/493.5/10

Moon/Mars/Space
Everything above y 1000 has no atmosphere/air, you have to use a spacesuit to survive there.
Also: torches won't stick and if you build a building there you have to make it air-thight (full blocks and corners) and use the air-pump (HV-machine)

Spaceship
You are encouraged to build your own spaceship for mining on the Moon and traveling among the stars. A basic layout and inspiration can be found on the BuckarooBanzai travelnet (https://pandorabox.io/map/#-1789.03125/472.5/13) at Station MSS Behemoth

Tutorial section
Also on the spawn platform is a Tutorial-teleporter with various player-contributed Tutorials (mesecons, space, etc)

Chat commands
  • /spawn teleports you spawn (shop at skyplatform)
  • /home /sethome
  • /mission_abort aborts the current mission (also in unified inv as button)
  • /tpr (playername) Teleport request to player
  • /tphr (playername) Teleport request to bring a player to your position
