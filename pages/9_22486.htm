Oh no, not another currency-mod ....

Of course, this comment is ok, but my mod has a special feature:
The coins are limited and not craftable.

There are 3 kind of coins: Copper, silver and gold and all of them can have their own amount on an server.
As admin, you can mine this coins from copper_ingot, tin_ingot or silver_ingot, if you have the mod moreores installed and at last gold_ingot.
You can melt coins to remove it from your server and if you lost or add coins on another way, you can set the amount by hand.

With a special privileg, coin_check, you can as admin nominate treasurer, they can then mine, melt or set the amount coins with you. Of course, this actions are logged.

Normal Player have the command:
/coins_show
This shows you the current amount of coins on the server, not the coins, who owned only by the player and
he get's a proposed exchange-rate.

The exchange-rate can set as a fix value or a dynamic value, depends on the value of coins, who are exists on the server.

To see it in action, i test it on the server Silicon Village (94.130.225.28:49152).
Try it and have fun with it.
