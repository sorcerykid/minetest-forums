Xblox is a simple light wight mod, that adds new blocks such as, Ocean themed blocks, smb blocks, and 3 types of plaster. this is my first mod, all textures done by me:) hope you enjoy!
License:
MIT License
Adds:
-Ocean Stone
-Ocean Cobble
-Ocean Brick
-Ocean Glass
-Carved Ocean Stone
-Sea Lantern
-Sand Cobblestone
-Compacted Obsidian
-Ground
-Brick
-Castle Brick
-Plasters
Download:
v2.0 https://github.com/starninjas/xblox

by parasite » Thu Mar 15, 2018 5:44 pm

Hi J1. This is very nice mod!

It offers a few blocks which looks very nice and have simple recipes that are based on very common things like dirt, cobble, water bucket or torch. So it adds to a game some new building materials and a new type of glass and lighting, and all that stuff will be available to player without much effort to craft in the early phase of the game, or - which I consider as equivalence - available during any phase of the game for fast and mass production for challenging and demanding construction projects or long roads. This mod is like modern, lightweight mini-moreblocks!

Although this mod is about only a few blocks, but that is all right. It can be used successfully as a small but powerful addition to any popular easy-fun server with many visually attractive and homedecor-like stuff - players will find something new, fresch and usefull to decorate their creations. Also it can be great update for all simple servers based on default minetest_game (and singleplayer gameplay), as this mod does not require any special materials for recipies.



Perhaps someone could complain here that the appearance of some blocks is too contrasting, too sharp, too visually expressive/agressive... but why not? It always can be changed and moreover, some may even like this. I also have a question. Do we need that hidden .git folder and its content in the mod folder?

And thanks for nice mod!
